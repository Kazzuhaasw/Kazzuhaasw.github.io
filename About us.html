Founded by a Harvard Master of Education alumna, Ace Academy believes that every educational gap can be closed and every challenge can be overcome with the right resources and guidance. Ms. Sophie, the founder, faced numerous challenges during her journey as a first-generation immigrant student, which fuels her passion for supporting others. At Ace Academy, we strive to empower families and student s to achieve their educational goals through our unique Ace Academy Method and exceptional team of instructors.
